Will Ward's Computer Help FAQ

Simple, friendly tech support answers

💻 Why is my computer so slow?

Most slow computers are caused by background programs, too many startup apps, or lack of updates. Try the following:

  • Restart your computer at least once a day
  • Uninstall apps you don’t use anymore
  • Don’t click on pop-ups or ads offering to "clean" your PC
  • If unsure, contact me and I can speed it up safely

🔑 I forgot my password, what do I do?

If it's a Windows login, try:

  • Click “Reset password” on the login screen
  • If you see a Microsoft email (like johnsmith@outlook.com), go to this page to reset it
  • If none of that works, I can help you recover or reset it

🛡️ I think I have a virus

Look for these signs:

  • Weird pop-ups or new toolbars
  • Apps you didn’t install showing up
  • Computer freezing or crashing more than usual

Turn off your computer and contact me, I use safe tools that don’t damage your files.

🖨️ My printer won’t print

Try this first:

  • Turn the printer off, wait 10 seconds, and turn it back on
  • Check if it’s connected to Wi-Fi or the USB cable
  • Make sure the printer is selected as the default one

Still stuck? I’ll help you get it going again.

📦 How do I back up my files?

  • Use a USB flash drive or external hard drive
  • Copy your folders like Documents, Pictures, Desktop, etc.
  • You can also use cloud storage like Google Drive or OneDrive

Don’t wait until it’s too late, back up your memories!

🔄 How do I reinstall Windows?

If your PC isn’t working right, reinstalling Windows might help, but it should be done carefully. I do this often for clients, and I can move your files safely.

💾 I’m running out of storage, what should I do?

Start by checking where your space is going:

  • Delete old downloads and large videos or photos you no longer need
  • Empty the Recycle Bin (right-click it on the desktop)
  • Uninstall programs you don’t use anymore
  • Use tools like “Storage Sense” on Windows to help clean things up

Still full? I can help clean it up safely or upgrade your drive.

🌐 Why won’t my Wi-Fi work?

Try this first:

  • Unplug your modem/router, wait 30 seconds, and plug it back in
  • Make sure your computer is connected to the right Wi-Fi name
  • Restart your computer and try again

If your internet still isn’t working, it could be the router or your provider. I can help figure it out.

📸 How do I move pictures from my phone to my computer?

  • Use the USB cable that came with your phone
  • Plug it into your computer, on most phones, you’ll need to tap “Allow” or “File Transfer”
  • Look for your phone in File Explorer under “This PC”
  • Drag and drop your pictures to the Pictures folder

If you’d rather use wireless tools or need help, I’ve got you covered.

🔇 I can’t hear any sound, how do I fix it?

  • Check the volume on your computer and speakers
  • Look for a small speaker icon in the bottom-right, click to raise the volume
  • Make sure your headphones or speakers are plugged in fully
  • Restart the computer if sound still doesn’t work

Still nothing? I’ll check the drivers and hardware for you.

🔌 How do I shut down my computer properly?

  • Click the Start button (bottom-left corner)
  • Click the power icon
  • Select “Shut Down”, this closes everything safely

Don’t just hold the power button unless it’s frozen. That can damage files.

🧹 How can I keep my computer healthy?

  • Restart it every day or two
  • Run updates when they pop up
  • Don’t click on sketchy ads or links
  • Back up your files every month
  • Keep food and drinks away from your keyboard!

It’s like brushing your teeth, small habits keep it running smooth.
